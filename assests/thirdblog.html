Top 5 Highest-Priced Cars Sold at Auction in 2024.



1.Ferrari 250 GTO (1962-1964)

The **Ferrari 250 GTO** is one of the most coveted and valuable cars ever built. Only 36 were made, and they dominate the upper echelon of auction sales.
- Auction Price: $70 million (private sale, 2018)
- Notable Sale: A 1962 Ferrari 250 GTO sold for **$48.4 million** at a 2018 RM Sotheby's auction, making it one of the most expensive cars ever sold publicly at auction.

2.Mercedes-Benz 300 SLR Uhlenhaut Coupé (1955)

In 2022, the Mercedes-Benz 300 SLR "Uhlenhaut Coupé" became the most expensive car ever sold at auction.
- Auction Price: €135 million ($143 million)
- Notable Sale: The car was sold by Mercedes-Benz in a private auction, making it the highest-priced car sale in history.

3.Bugatti Type 57SC Atlantic (1936-1938)

One of the rarest cars in the world, the Bugatti Type 57SC Atlantic is iconic for its art-deco styling and exceptional performance for its time.
Auction Price: Estimated at over $40 million
Notable Sale: Only two original models exist today, and one has been privately sold for around $40 million.

4.Ferrari 335 Sport Scaglietti (1957)

This racing legend, which competed in numerous Grand Prix races, is another Ferrari that tops the charts.
Auction Price: $35.7 million (2016)
Notable Sale: A 1957 Ferrari 335 Sport Scaglietti sold at an Artcurial auction in 2016 for €32.1 million ($35.7 million), setting a record at the time.

5.Ferrari 290 MM (1956)

Built for the Mille Miglia race, the Ferrari 290 MM is a rare racing car with a prestigious history.
Auction Price: $22 million (2018)
Notable Sale: A 1956 Ferrari 290 MM sold for **$22 million** at a RM Sotheby’s auction in 2018, solidifying Ferrari’s dominance in high-end auction sales.
