Top 10 “Movies and webseries” in hollywood in my choice.



1. Breaking Bad (2008-2013)

Few TV shows have managed to captivate audiences the way Breaking Bad did. This gritty crime drama follows the transformation of Walter White, a high school chemistry teacher turned meth kingpin. It's a masterclass in character development, storytelling, and tension-building, with Bryan Cranston’s portrayal of White remaining one of the most iconic performances in television history.

2. Avengers: Endgame (2019)

A decade in the making, Avengers: Endgame is a monumental film in the Marvel Cinematic Universe, wrapping up numerous storylines that had been building since Iron Man in 2008. This epic conclusion to the Infinity Saga showcases teamwork, sacrifice, and heroism as the Avengers face their greatest challenge yet: defeating Thanos and reversing the catastrophic Snap..

3. The Dark Knight (2008)

Christopher Nolan’s The Dark Knight is more than just a superhero film—it's a gripping crime thriller that dives into the psyche of its characters. Heath Ledger’s Oscar-winning portrayal of the Joker is nothing short of legendary, creating one of cinema’s most chilling villains. The movie expertly balances action with profound questions about chaos, morality, and justice.

4. Avatar (2009)

James Cameron’s Avatar was a groundbreaking achievement in visual effects and 3D technology, transporting audiences to the lush, alien world of Pandora. The film's environmental themes, combined with its stunning visuals, offer an immersive cinematic experience that made it the highest-grossing movie for nearly a decade.

5. Stranger Things (2016-Present)

Set in the 1980s, Stranger Things blends supernatural horror with coming-of-age drama, making it a nostalgic yet fresh series for both new audiences and fans of retro pop culture. With a talented cast of young actors and a thrilling narrative revolving around alternate dimensions and government conspiracies, it has become a cultural phenomenon.

6. Ford v Ferrari (2019)

This exhilarating film tells the true story of the rivalry between car manufacturers Ford and Ferrari in the 1960s, culminating in the legendary 24 Hours of Le Mans race. With standout performances from Christian Bale and Matt Damon, Ford v Ferrari is both a high-speed thrill ride and an emotional exploration of passion, friendship, and the cost of ambition

7. Lamborghini: The Man Behind the Legend (2022)

Lamborghini: The Man Behind the Legend offers a biographical look into the life of Ferruccio Lamborghini, the mastermind behind the luxury car brand. It explores his journey from a tractor manufacturer to creating one of the most iconic supercars in the world. This movie dives deep into his personal and professional life, showcasing his relentless ambition and vision.

8. Vikings (2013-2020)

Vikings offers a raw and gripping look into Norse history and mythology, chronicling the lives of legendary Viking chieftains like Ragnar Lothbrok and his sons. The show combines epic battles, rich character arcs, and historical intrigue to create a gripping narrative filled with betrayal, conquest, and exploration.

9. Money Heist (La Casa de Papel) (2017-2021)

Money Heist, a Spanish crime drama, has gained international acclaim for its thrilling heist sequences, complex characters, and nail-biting tension. Following a group of robbers who plan meticulously executed heists, led by the enigmatic "Professor," the show explores themes of rebellion, loyalty, and survival..

10.Mission: Impossible – Dead Reckoning Part One (2023)

The most recent entry in the series, Dead Reckoning Part One, directed once again by Christopher McQuarrie, is the first part of a two-part story that raises the stakes to new heights. This time, Ethan and his team must confront a new global threat that endangers their mission and lives. The film is packed with action, including a thrilling train sequence and a motorcycle stunt that sees Cruise launching himself off a cliff, a real stunt performed by Cruise himself
